About

FCC MAN is a cohort that focuses on helping new coders and veteran coders alike. This group will create an environment where everyone can freely share their thoughts and opinions, or ask for help if they need it. This method of learning is ideal for people who are too shy or just overwhelmed by forums and other chat boxes with a lot of members. This group aims to have at least 30 members who are active in helping those who have some problems in code and willing to contribute to some open source projects.

Joining this group you will give you the chance to share your ideas with a smaller group of people who are willing to listen and help you turn that idea into a reality.

FCC MAN is still a new group and is happily accepting new members that are eager to learn or contribute to open source projects.

Resources

This group will study coding, mainly Web Development based on the FCC curriculum which is the main reason this group is formed.

But we will have some resources outside Free Code Camp that will supplement our learning.

This group will mostly focus on the resources available at the internet which are mostly free.
